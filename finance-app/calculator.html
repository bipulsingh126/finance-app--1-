What is a Personal Loan EMI Calculator?

An EMI Calculator helps you find out the exact monthly installment you need to pay on your loan. Just enter your Loan Amount, Interest Rate, and Loan Tenure — the calculator will instantly show your EMI and total interest payable.

How to Use?

  1. Loan Amount: Enter or adjust the amount you wish to borrow.
  2. Loan Tenure: Select the repayment period in months or years.
  3. Interest Rate: Enter the applicable rate of interest.
  4. The calculator will automatically display your monthly EMI and overall interest.

Why Use Our EMI Calculator?

  • ✔ Quick and accurate results
  • ✔ Helps you know your EMI in advance
  • ✔ Choose the right tenure for your budget
  • ✔ Better planning for monthly expenses
